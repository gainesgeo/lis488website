About Me

I'm so glad you're here! I'm Georgie, and I'm an aspiring photograph archivist. This website outlines all of my education, professional experience, research projects, and personal interests that have prepared me for this career path. Please feel free to reach out with questions or opportunities to connect.

I created this site to fulfill the requirements for LIS 488: Technology for Information Professionals, as part of earning my master's degree in library and information science at Simmons University. That is to say, it has been created by the html and css skills I've acquired through the course of the class.
